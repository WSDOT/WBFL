SegmentCrossSection Object

An object defining the material and geometric properties of a segment cross section

Properties

Area

Sets/Gets the cross sectional area

CTE

Sets/Gets the coefficient of thermal expansion

DeflectionMomentOfInertia

Sets/Gets the moment of inertia used for deflection calculations

Density

Sets/Gets the density of the material

Depth

Sets/Gets the depth of the cross section

E

Sets/Gets the modulus of elasticity

MomentOfInertia

Sets/Gets the moment of inertia

SelfWgtArea

Sets/Gets the area used for computing self weight

StressPoints

Returns a collection of stress points

Events

OnSegmentCrossSectionChanged

Fires when the cross section changes
