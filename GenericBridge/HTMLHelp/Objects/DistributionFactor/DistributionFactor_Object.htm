DistributionFactor Object

An object that represents a set of live load distribution factors at a point of interest in the structure.

Properties

GDMul

Get/Set distribution factor for deflection with 2 or more lanes loaded

GDSgl

Get/Set distribution factor for deflection with one lane loaded

GFat

Get/Set distribution factor for fatigue

GNMMul

Get/Set distribution factor for negative moment with 2 or more lanes loaded

GNMSgl

Get/Set distribution factor for negative moment with one lane loaded

GPMMul

Get/Set distribution factor for positive moment with 2 or more lanes loaded

GPMSgl

Get/Set distribution factor for positive moment with one lane loaded

GRMul

Get/Set distribution factor for reaction with 2 or more lanes loaded

GRSgl

Get/Set distribution factor for reaction with one lane loaded

GTMul

Get/Set distribution factor for rotation with 2 or more lanes loaded

GTSgl

Get/Set distribution factor for rotation with one lane loaded

GVMul

Get/Set distribution factor for shear with 2 or more lanes loaded

GVSgl

Get/Set distribution factor for shear with one lane loaded

Methods

GetG

Gets all of the distribution factors at once

SetG

Sets all of the distribution factors at once

Events

OnDistributionFactorChanged

Fired when the distribution factor changes
