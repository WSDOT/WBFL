StressPoint Object

A stress point is a point on a cross section where stresses are computed. The StressPoint object defines the properties for stress calculations. Stresses are computed as

f = (P)(Sa) + (M)(Sm)

where

P is the axial force
M is the moment
Sa is the axial force stress coefficient (typically 1/Area)
Sm is the bending stress coefficient (typically 1/I)

Properties

Sa

Sets/Gets the axial force stress coefficient

Sm

Sets/Gets the bending stress coefficient

Events

OnStressPointChanged

Fires when the stress point changes
