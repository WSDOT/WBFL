Station Object

An object that represents a station. A Station is defined by its StationZoneIndex and its Value. The StationZoneIndex describes which in StationEquation zone the Station is located. The number of StationEquation zones is always one greater that the number of station equations. The Value of a Station is its location within the station equation zone.

Consider an alignment that has a station equation of 3+00 Back = 7+00 Ahead and 14+00 Back = 9+00 Ahead. A station at 10+00 is not uniquely identified because it can occur in two locations. The station is uniquely identified when its StationZoneIndex is used. Station 10+00 in station zone 1 is uniquely identified.

A station can also be represented by its normalized value. Its normalized value is the location of the station without considering station equations. Station 10+00 in Station Zone 1 is 300 ft past the first station equation and has a normalized value of 6+00.
