IIntersect2 Interface

An interface used to create points intersecting lines are arcs.

Methods

BearingCircle

Finds the intersection of a line and a circle

Bearings

Finds the intersection of two lines defined by bearings

Circles

Finds the intersection of two circles

LineByPointsCircle

Finds the intersection of a line defined by two points and a circle

Lines

Finds the intersection of two stored line segments

LinesByPoints

Finds the intersection of two lines defined by end points

LineSegmentCircle

Finds the intersection of a stored line segment and a circle
