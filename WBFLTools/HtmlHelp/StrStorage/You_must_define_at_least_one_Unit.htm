You must define at least one Unit

You must define at least one Unit before you can save properties.

You must have one, and only one, top-level unit. Units are defined when saving by calling BeginUnit/EndUnit.

All structured storage documents must start with a call to BeginUnit and end with a call to EndUnit. You cannot save properties until you have at least one Unit defined. And, all properties and sub-Units must be enclosed within the top-level unit.
