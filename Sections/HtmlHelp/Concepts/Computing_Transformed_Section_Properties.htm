Computing Transformed Section Properties

Often time structural engineering application, properties of composite, non-homogenous, sections must be transformed into properties of a homogenous section. This is easily accomodated with the TransformProperties method of the ElasticProperties object.

There are two methods for computing transformed properties.

  1. Use the actual modulus of elasticity of the items that make up section. Then, supply the TransformedProperties method the modulus of elasticity of the material into which the properties will be transformed.

  2. Use the modular ratio (n) in place of the modulus of elasticty for each item that makes up the section. Supply TransformedProperties a modulus of elasticity value of 1.0.
