R Property (ManderModel Object)

Summary:

Sets/Gets the ratio of increase strain a peak stress to increase in strength at peak stress due to confinement.

Syntax:

dblR = object.R
object.R = dblR

Element

Description

dblR

Ratio of increase strain a peak stress to increase in strength at peak stress due to confinement

object

This object

Remarks:

Generally varies from 3 to 6 with a typical value of 5.
