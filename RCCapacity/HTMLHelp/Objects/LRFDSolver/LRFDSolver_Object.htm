LRFDSolver Object

This object implements the IRCSolver interface to solve reinforced concrete moment capacity problems. The method of solution is based on the equations published in Article 5.7.3 of the AASHTO LRFD Bridge Design Specifications, 2nd Edition, 1998 with interim revisions through 2002.
