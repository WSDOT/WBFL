CircularManderSection Object

This object implements the IManderModelSection interface and represents a circular column with concentric longitudinal reinforcement and  transverse reinforcement of either spirals or hoops.

Properties

As

Set/Get the area of longitudinal reinforcement

Asp

Set/Get the area of transverse reinforcement

Cover

Set/Get the clear cover

db

Set/Get the diameter of the transverse reinforcement

Diameter

Set/Get the column diameter

S

Set/Get the spacing of the transverse reinforcement

TransvReinforcementType

Set/Get the transverse reinforcement type
