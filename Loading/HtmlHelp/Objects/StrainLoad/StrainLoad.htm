StrainLoad

A strain load applied to a member in the LBAM.

StrainLoad objects are stored in the StrainLoads collection.

Be aware that most analysis engines use small displacement assumptions. Large strains will likely cause large errors.

AxialStrain

Axial strain applied to the member

CurvatureStrain

Curvature strain applied to the member

MemberID

ID of member this StrainLoad is attached to

MemberType

Type of member StrainLoad is attached to

Methods

Clone

Creates a clone of this StrainLoad

Events

OnStrainLoadChanged

Fired whenever a change is made to the StrainLoad or its parts
