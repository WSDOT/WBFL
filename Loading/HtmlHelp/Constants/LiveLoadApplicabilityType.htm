LiveLoadApplicabilityType

Designates whether live load results are applicable over the entire structure, or restricted to certain conditions.

Constant

Value

Description

llaEntireStructure

0

All live load results are applicable over entire structure.

llaContraflexure

1

Live load results are applicable only for reactions at interior supports, and for negative moment in areas of dead load contraflexure.
