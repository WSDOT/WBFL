Cumulative responses may not be requested for internally generated POIs. POI ID's must be positive when requesting cumulative results

The LBAM analysis engine automatically creates internally generated POIs if there are not enough POI's to create influence lines. These POI's are given negative ID's.

The number and location of internally generated POI's will typically vary through Stages. This means that an internally generated POI may not exist in all Stages, or may not be at the same location for all Stages. Hence, we cannot compute cumulative results for them.
