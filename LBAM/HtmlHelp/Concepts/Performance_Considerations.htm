Performance Considerations

The LBAM Analysis engines build structural (F.E.) models of the LBAM automatically. Hence, you have little control over many aspects of the model building. However, there are some steps that can be taken to improve performance:

  • Only create as many POI's as you need. When results are requested, most of the analysis engines batch their computations and then cache the results. This means that computations will be performed for all POI's on the structure - regardless of wheter or not results are requested at them.

  • Use arrays of POI's when requesting results. The reponse computation methods are optimized for this case.

  • Only create as many member loads as you need. The system will perform an analysis of all LoadGroups that contain loads. LoadGroups without loads are not analyzed.
