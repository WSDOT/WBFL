Describing Distribution Factors

Along the Superstructure

The LBAM allows live load distribution factors to vary in prismatic segments along the length of the superstructure. Distribution factors are defined by adding DistributionFactorSegment objects to the DistributionFactors collection.

The DistributionFactors collection describes segments starting from the left end of the left overhang, if present.

NOTE:If the DistributionFactors collection is empty (this is the default), then all distribution factors default to a value of 1.0 (i.e., one axle of live load is applied).

At Supports

Each Support or TemporarySupport object in the model has a DistributionFactor object associated with it. These distribution factors are used to compute reactions at the support and member actions along the entire support.
