Load Combination Hierarchy

The LBAM provides a flexible, model for applying loads, grouping them, and combining force results. As shown in the figure, there are four levels.

  1. Member loads, such as PointLoads and DistributedLoads are applied along SuperStructureMembers, Spans, Supports, or TemporarySupports. SettlementLoads SettlementLoads may also be applied to Supports

  2. Each of the loads from (1) are assigned to a LoadGroup and a Stage.

  3. The results of one or more LoadGroups are summed together to create LoadCase results.

  4. The results from a LiveLoadModel and one or more LoadCases are factored and summed together to create LoadCombination results.

 

Note that LoadGroup results can be persistent through all Stages or they can be transient (i.e., applicable only to a single Stage).
