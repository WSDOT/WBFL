GetSupportDistributionFactor Method (IGetDistributionFactors)

Summary:

Gets the DistributionFactor(s) obect at a given Support

Syntax:

objDistributionFactor = IGetDistributionFactors.GetSupportDistributionFactor([in]intSupportID, [in]bstrStage)

Element

Description

objDistributionFactor

The DistributionFactor object at the support

intSupportID

ID or index of the desired support

bstrStage

The desired stage

IGetDistributionFactors

The IGetDistributionFactors object

Remarks:

Permanent supports are requested by their index (order) in the Suports collection. TemporarySupports are requested by their unique ID.
