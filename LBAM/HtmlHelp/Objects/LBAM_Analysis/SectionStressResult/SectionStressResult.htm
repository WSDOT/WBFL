SectionStressResult

Represents the stress results at a section. This object has two collections of stress results - one for the left side of the section and another for the right side of the section. The order of the results correlates to the order of the StressPoints at the location.

Properties

LeftCount

Number of stress results on the left section

RightCount

Number of stress results on the right section
