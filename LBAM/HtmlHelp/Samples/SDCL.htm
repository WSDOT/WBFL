SDCL.xls

This sample is an Excel spreadsheet that demonstrates a simple for dead load, continuous for live load analysis for a superstructure with up to five spans. This type of analysis is typical of slab-on-girder bridges and uses three stages of analysis. The first stage is used to model the freshly-placed wet slab on the girders. The section stage is used to model the placement of the wearing surface and traffic barriers onto the composite section. And, the third and final stage is used for the live load analysis.

The spreadsheet demonstrates many of the feature of the LBAM and computes results for LoadGroups, LoadCases, LiveLoads, and pertinant LRFD load combinations.
