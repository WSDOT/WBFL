Force Sign Conventions

Member Forces

The sign conventions for member force output are shown below. The forces returned are the externally applied forces required to hold the member in equilibrium.

 

POI Forces

POI forces can be requested at either side of the location where the POI cuts the member. The forces returned are the externally applied forces required to hold the member in equilibrium.. POI forces can be specified in local member coordinates or global coordinates.

 

Note that this convention creates a slight discrepancy when a POI is located at the end of the member: one of the faces does not exist. For instance, the left face does not exist on the starting face of the member. For consistency, the forces returned in these cases will be equal and opposite to the true face at the end of the member.

Reaction Forces

Reaction forces are returned in global coordinates. The forces returned are the forces applied to the model at the support point.
