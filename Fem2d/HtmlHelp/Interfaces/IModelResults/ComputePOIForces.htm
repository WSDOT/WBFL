ComputePOIForces Method

(IModelResults Interface)

Summary:

Computes the forces at a given POI for a given loading

Syntax:

object.ComputePOIForces( [in]intLC, [in]intPOId, [in]intFace, [in]intOrientation, [out]fltFx, [out]fltFy, [out]fltMz)

Element

Description

intMbrId

ID of member where forces are desired

intLC

ID of Loading

intFace

Constant of type f2dMbrFaceType requesting which face to compute member forces on.

intOrientation

Constant of type f2dLoadOrientation requesting results in Member or Global coordinates

fltFx

Force in x direction at POI

fltFy

Force in x direction at POI

fltMz

Moment in z direction at POI

object

This Model

Remarks:

The forces returned are the internal forces within the member.
