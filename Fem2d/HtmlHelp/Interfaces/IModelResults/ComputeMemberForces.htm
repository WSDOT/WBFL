ComputeMemberForces Method

(IModelResults Interface)

Summary:

Computes the forces at the ends of a given member for a given loading

Syntax:

object.ComputeMemberForces([in]intLC, [in]intMbrId, [out]fltStartFx, [out]fltStartFy, [out]fltStartMz, [out]fltEndFx, [out]fltEndFy, [out]fltEndMz)

Element

Description

IintLC

ID of Loading

intMbrId

ID of member where displacements are desired

fltStartFx

Force in x direction at start of beam

fltStartFy

Force in x direction at start of beam

fltStartMz

Moment in z direction at start of beam

fltEndFx

Force in x direction at end of beam

fltEndFy

Force in x direction at end of beam

fltEndMz

Moment in z direction at end of beam

object

This Model

Remarks:

Forces returned are in member coordinates
